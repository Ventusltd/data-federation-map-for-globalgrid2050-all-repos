# Spider Eyes And Senses

Provenance: declared by the principal, worded by the auditor, committed manually by the principal. Source SHA to be stamped by the documentation-hygiene discipline.

Companion files: SPIDER_SENSORY_SYSTEM.md defines the vocabulary. every-drop-is-the-ocean/SPIDER_BOUNDLESS_CHARTER.md defines the reach, legality and beneficiaries. This file defines how an eye and a sense actually work and how one is born.

## What an eye is and how it works

An eye is a geospatial rendering app: a page that draws the physical world. The proven specimen is the REPD grid atlas, a self-contained organism of one shell, one engine, one stylesheet and dozens of local geojson cartridges covering the 11kV to 400kV networks, substations, planning projects, chargers, data centres, roads, rail, ports and subsea routes.

An eye works by the cartridge law. Its layer registry is a table of rows, each row declaring a layer's identity, label, colour, geometry, data path, filter and behaviour, and one generic engine expresses every row. The eye reads committed local data, invents nothing, fails loudly when a layer is missing, and states its attributions on its face. An eye may carry drill paths from map object to detail view, and it may export what it shows, with the screening-grade label travelling on the export.

The Spider does not reinvent an eye. It federates one. The eye's registry becomes a declared child cartridge; the eye's external dependencies become declared edges to source nodes; the eye's served page becomes a surface node whose health is charted honestly, live, degraded or gone. The Spider sees the data and the process behind the eye and builds the links; the eye keeps doing the rendering it already does well.

## What a sense is and how it works

A sense is a time-series or market-intelligence app: a page that perceives change. The proven specimen is the UK energy tracking family, which reads settlement prices back through the years, transmission-metered generation by technology, stored solar energy by day and season, interconnector net flows by country, and reconciles the totals.

A sense works by the pipeline law. Behind each chart is a declared chain: source, fetch, store, transform, render. A well-formed sense states its own chain on its face, as the tracker already does in its inputs, mechanism and outputs box, and its source-transparency notes. That statement is a manifest in embryo. A sense never blends live and stored without saying which is which, never presents a derived rollup as a metered fact, and carries its high and low markers as honest extremes, not decoration.

The Spider federates a sense the same way it federates an eye: the pipeline chain becomes declared edges from source node through data repo to surface; the app's own transparency box becomes its declared manifest; its charts remain its own business.

## How an eye or a sense is born into the federation

The birth of a sensory organ is a fixed loop with five steps, and every step is receipted.

First, declaration. The hosting repo or app carries a manifest stating what it is, one of eye_app, sense_app, external_source or served_surface, what data it reads, which sources feed it and which surface serves it. Where a manifest exists, the Spider reads it as declared local fact. Where none exists yet, the scanner may infer the same facts from code and registries, and everything so inferred is derived until the principal's dispatch records it as declared.

Second, smell. The scanner detects the new or changed thing: a fresh repository, a new entry in the target registry, a manifest appearing where none was.

Third, see. The scanner walks the structure: layer registries, data folders, fetch scripts, endpoint references, served pages, and emits candidate nodes, edges and a child cartridge, all in audit mode, touching no truth.

Fourth, chart. On the principal's dispatch, and only then, the candidate becomes committed: the organ appears in the radial as a node with its kind badge, its edges to hosts, cartridges, sources and surfaces, and a Contents drill into its insides. Scheduled runs may look; only dispatch may chart.

Fifth, receipt. The auditor clean-clones the landing commit and reproduces the checks: every entry resolves to a real path or endpoint, provenance is declared-only where claimed, the base store is byte-identical outside scope, and generation is deterministic. The closing SHA is recorded.

## The first organs

The first eye to federate is the REPD grid atlas, whose registry already reads like DNA and whose known port wrinkle, the handful of root-relative paths reaching into the monolith, becomes the port checklist. The first sense is the UK energy tracker, whose pipelines already touch Elexon and the solar feed and whose transparency boxes are manifests waiting to be formalised. Both arrive as child scopes the existing renderer can draw without learning a new trick, which is the whole point: extending sight is rows and receipts, not engineering.

## One sentence

An eye draws the world from cartridges, a sense perceives change through declared pipelines, and either is born into the web by declaration, smell, seeing, the principal's dispatch and the auditor's receipt, so that every new organ of sight arrives already unable to lie.
