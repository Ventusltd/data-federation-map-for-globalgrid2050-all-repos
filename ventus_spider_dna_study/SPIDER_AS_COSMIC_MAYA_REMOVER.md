## Subtitle

## What Maya Means Here

Maya means false appearance.

In the GlobalGrid2050 federation, maya appears when something looks clear but is not actually proven.

A dashboard can look live while reading a snapshot.

A repo can look complete while missing a manifest.

A workflow can look successful while not being independently receipted.

A report can look like proof while only being evidence.

A source can look accepted while still only candidate.

A relationship line can look authoritative while lacking declared provenance.

A public page can look polished while the data behind it is stale.

The Spider exists to remove this false appearance.
